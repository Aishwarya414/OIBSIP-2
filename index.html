Sushant Singh Rajput

Sushant Singh Rajput: who made a successful transition from Television to films.Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor. He started his acting career with television serials. His first serial was Kis Desh Main Hai Meraa Dil. After that, he acted in Zee TV’s popular soap opera Pavitra Rishta.

About the Actor

☛ Rajput was born on 21st January 1986 in Patna. His parents are Krishna Kumar Singh and Usha Singh. Her mother died in 2002. Same year his family moved to Delhi from Patna. Mitu Singh, his sister is a state-level cricketer. He completed his school education from St. Karen’s high school, Patna and Kulachi Hansraj Model School New Delhi.
